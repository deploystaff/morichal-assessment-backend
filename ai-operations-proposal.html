How do you monitor our orders?

We track every transaction flowing through the platform - emails received, documents processed, CRM syncs completed. If something gets stuck or fails, we get an alert and address it before it impacts your business.

What kind of reports will we receive?

You'll see order processing speed, document extraction accuracy, bottleneck locations, and throughput trends. Essential gets monthly reports; Professional gets weekly; Enterprise gets real-time dashboard access.

How often do you train our team?

Essential includes quarterly training sessions. Professional includes monthly sessions. Enterprise gets unlimited training - we're always available to onboard new staff or refresh your team's skills.

What happens if orders stop processing?

Our 24/7 monitoring catches this immediately. We diagnose the issue and resolve it - often before you even notice. Response times depend on your plan: 24h Essential, 12h Professional, 4h Enterprise.

What counts as a "development hour"?

New features, workflow changes, report customization, tool adjustments for your team. Bug fixes and maintenance are always included regardless of hours - that's our job.
